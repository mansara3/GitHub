Course information

Business information Technology is an excellent course choice for anyone who has a passion for both business and IT. Business information technology BSc hons is a course that best prepares you for meeting challenges of the work environment as you will be taught the required academic and industrial knowledge. The course teaches you both the fundamentals of both business and information technology.

Enhances employability and technical skills

The course will provide you with the practical, technical and academic skills that will help you pursue your chosen job. The course delivers the use of advanced software development and computer programming labs, as well as dedicated facilities for systems analysis, artificial intelligence, e-commerce and business intelligence.

You will apply these core themes to specific modules, such as real world projects where you will work as part of a development team to deliver a business IT solution. You will also have the chance to apply for a work placement or study abroad where you will put your theory to the test.

Graduate options

Students of this course have also gone on to work for such high-end companies as Hewlett Packard, Bell Micro, BT, Cisco, Deloitte, Ericsson, Fujitsu, IBM, Intel Corporation, Siemens and Nokia.
With the right supports in looking for jobs or placements business information technology students typically pursue careers such as:

Business analysts, business application developers, database designers and IS consultants. Furthermore of roles include Change managers, business architects and CRM consultants.
